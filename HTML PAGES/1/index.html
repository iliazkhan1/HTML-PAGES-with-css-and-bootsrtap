SRM AP

March 14, 2025

SRM University-AP (SRMAP), established in 2017 in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh,
is a multi-stream research university offering undergraduate, postgraduate,
and doctoral programs in fields like engineering, liberal arts, and management,
with a focus on becoming a globally connected, nationally relevant,
and regionally transformative institution.

SRM RAMAPURAM

March 5, 2025

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) Ramapuram,
established in 2004, is a prominent university in Chennai,
Tamil Nadu, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate,
and doctoral programs in engineering, management, medicine,
science, and humanities.

About

April 1, 2025

SRM Institute of Science and Technology is one of the top ranking universities
in India with over 52,000 full time students and more than 3200 faculty
across all the campuses – Kattankulathur, Ramapuram, Vadapalani Campus – all
in and around Chennai.
